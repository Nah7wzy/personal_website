A Timeless Classic



12 Angry Men

This was an absolute cracker to watch! Definitely a top 3 of all time movie. when the entire movie is set in one room and to achieve such a thrill is remarkable. The conversations are the strongest quality of this movie that keeps the viewer excited. Further more, it accomodated a brilliant acting by each character. It portrays a combination of rational thinking mixed with rushed and swayed decisions.
