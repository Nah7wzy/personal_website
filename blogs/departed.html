The Departed

Tw Fb In

From begining to end this is one of the most entertaining movies of post 2000. What stands out for me in this movie is the strange plot display that don't seem to follow movie logic, hence making it a very realistic work. It matched the expectation of brilliant acting as it compromised the most talented actors from the stars to the minor roles. More specifically I found the performance of Leonardo dicaprio truly remarkable, as always. This is a must see for all movie fans
